Our Story

Crafted With Passion

RoyalTaste was born from a simple idea: bring world-class fine dining to everyone. What started as a small 20-seat restaurant in 2011 has grown into one of the most celebrated dining destinations.

Our founder, Chef Antonio, trained in Paris and Milan before returning home to create a menu that blends global techniques with local ingredients. Every dish is a story.
